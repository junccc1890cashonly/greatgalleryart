Lovart Prompt

Create a modern editorial lifestyle scene with soft daylight, quiet luxury atmosphere, restrained neutral tones, tactile materials, subtle texture contrasts, and generous negative space. The composition should feel calm, curated, and premium rather than decorative, with elegant framing, understated styling, and a refined visual rhythm suitable for a contemporary design-forward brand campaign.
